Retiquette: Etiquette for new Racketeers

Racketeers are expected to live up to the following rules:
  1. read the style guide and gradually absorb its recommendations;
  2. re-assign bug reports only after they can eliminate their own code (e.g., a collects) from the report; the best way to accomplish this goal is to create a new example that re-creates the problem without involvement of their code;
Matthias Felleisen
